## 💭 Overall Reflection

### What I Gained This Semester

This semester in SECP3843 — Special Topics in Data Engineering — was a comprehensive exposure to the modern data engineering landscape. I worked with cloud platforms (Azure), big data frameworks (Apache Spark), and AI tools, all within the context of real industry engagement.

The combination of assignments, industry talk, industry visit, and projects gave me a 360° view: theoretical foundations, hands-on technical skills, professional context, and practical application.

### Key Takeaways

- **Cloud is no longer optional** — Azure showed me how central cloud infrastructure is to modern data workflows.
- **Scale matters** — Apache Spark reframed my thinking from single-machine to distributed processing.
- **AI is a tool, not magic** — Understanding the pipeline behind AI systems made me a more grounded practitioner.
- **Industry != classroom** — The visit and industry project showed that real-world data is messier and decisions more complex than textbook problems.

### Looking Forward

I aim to pursue cloud certifications (Azure Fundamentals / Data Engineer Associate) and deepen my big data skills. I also want to contribute to open-source data engineering projects to build a stronger public portfolio.
